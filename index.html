Meet Patricia

Hi! I'm a certified Pilates instructor with years of hands-on experience helping people move better, feel stronger, and reconnect with their bodies. I’ve worked with everyone from first-timers to athletes, focusing on alignment, strength, and long-term mobility.

My sessions combine classical technique with a modern, no-fluff approach. It’s about progress you can feel — and actually enjoy. Whether we’re on the mat or the reformer, you’ll leave every session knowing you showed up for yourself — and that’s what it’s all about.
